FAQ

Q. Which customers qualify for this special offer? Qualifying Pre-selected out-of-contract MTN consumer customers only, who are eligible for an upgrade.

Q. How long is the promotional offer valid for? The promotion is valid from 16 September 2016 until 31 January 2017, qualifying customers who perform a successful upgrade during this period will benefit from the promotion

Q. How many times does the customer receive the free additional value? Eligible MTN customers will receive the additional 10 000 MTN-to-MTN minutes and 50GB data bundles every month for the next 24 months of their contract.

Q. Will I receive all my additional free value on upgrade? Prorata rules apply to the additional free value for the 1st month of the new upgrade contract. From the next bill run the customer will receive the full value.

Q. What is the valifity of the additional free value? The additional free value is valid for 30 days only, and there is no carry over.

Q. Will I lose the additional free value if I do a migration? No, if the customer does a migration but maintains or increases their monthly spend, they will keep the free value (My MTNChoice, My MTNChoice+, and MTN Anytime TopUp).

Q. Will customers receive loyalty points for the free additional value? No, customers will not receive loyalty points for the use of the free additional value.
